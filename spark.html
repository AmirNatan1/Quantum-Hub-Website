what it is

Partners pick. We execute.

A batch-based runway where our partners — not us — select the startups whose solutions fit a concrete use case inside their companies. Quantum's team handles scouting, execution, management, and accompaniment throughout.

faqs

Before you ask

What does it cost to join? Do you take equity?

The program is equity-free and we take no fees.

Who can apply?

Innovative companies in our industries: a working product at MVP/beta stage or beyond, a dedicated full-time team, and TRL 5+. Companies at different funding stages are welcome; the program fits post-seed startups best.

What are the selection criteria?

Concrete interest from our partners in a specific use case, a designated champion, and adequate resources to execute a POC within a relatively short period.

What if we apply and don't get in?

Every application is reviewed by Quantum's experts. A solution that doesn't fit today may fit a future batch — we encourage reapplying.

We applied to a previous batch — can we reapply?

Absolutely. Outline what you've achieved since your last application so we can assess your progress.

What commitment is required?

Accepted startups commit their best efforts to work with the program's team and partners, and to participate in the sessions agreed at the start of the program.

What happens after the program?

Collaborations often continue past the batch — POC execution can outlast the program, and alumni stay relevant for other partners. Alumni keep access to the ecosystem.
