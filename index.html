who we are

A matchmaker with a workshop

Quantum-hub is the shared innovation arm for four corporate partners. Across Automotive, Logistics, Industry 4.0, and Energy, our team scouts solutions against partner-defined needs and proves the strongest matches in a hands-on POC environment.

how we work

From pain point to pilot, end to end

1 · We start with the need

Our partners' real pain points guide the search — commercial need first, technology second. That's why our matches hold.

2 · We scout and vet

We screen startups globally against each use case — stage, tech readiness, and a partner with a concrete interest.

3 · We prove it with a POC

Design, planning, and execution at our own playground — without interrupting anyone's daily operations.

4 · We scale what works

A validated POC becomes a pilot, and a pilot becomes a commercial partnership. We stay until it does.

We prove the fit before partners commit to rollout.
