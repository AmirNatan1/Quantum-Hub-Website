Accessibility statement

This statement describes the accessibility provisions in the current website.

Our commitment

We believe the internet should be available and accessible to anyone, and we are committed to providing a website that is accessible to the broadest possible audience, regardless of ability. This website aims to conform to the World Wide Web Consortium's Web Content Accessibility Guidelines 2.1 at level AA.

What this site does

  • Keyboard navigation: every interactive element — navigation, dropdowns, accordions, filters, and forms — is operable by keyboard, with a clearly visible focus indicator on every focusable element. Dropdowns and overlays close with Esc.
  • Reduced motion: all scroll animations, pinned sections, count-ups, and background effects are disabled automatically for users whose system requests reduced motion (prefers-reduced-motion); content renders in its final state.
  • Screen readers: semantic landmarks (header, nav, main, footer), a single H1 per page, labeled form fields, ARIA attributes on menus and dialogs, and alt text on images. Animated statistics expose their final values as static text.
  • Color and contrast: text colors on the dark theme are chosen to meet WCAG AA contrast ratios; color is never the only carrier of meaning.
  • Video: background video plays muted, never autoplays audio, and offers a pause control.
  • Touch targets: interactive elements meet minimum touch-target sizes on mobile.

Known limitations

Despite our best efforts, some pages or sections may not yet be fully accessible, may be in the process of becoming accessible, or may lack an adequate technological solution. We are continually improving.

Feedback

If you encounter an accessibility barrier on this website, please tell us and we will do our best to fix it: intern@quantum-hub.com
