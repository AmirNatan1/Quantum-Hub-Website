What cookies are

Cookies are small files placed on your device by a website, used to remember your preferences and to understand how the site is used. Details of how we handle the data involved are in our privacy policy.

Cookies this site uses

  • Strictly necessary: session cookies required for core site functions (e.g. remembering a submitted form state within a visit).
  • Functionality: your language preference and your Partners/Startups choice on the home page, stored locally so the site can tailor what it emphasizes.
  • Analytics (if enabled): third-party analytics such as Google Analytics, to understand site usage. You can opt out via browser settings or the vendor's opt-out tools.

Managing cookies

You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. If you do not accept cookies, some parts of the site may not function fully.
