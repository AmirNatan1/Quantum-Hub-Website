What cookies are

Cookies are small files placed on your device by a website, used to remember your preferences and to understand how the site is used. Details of how we handle the data involved are in our privacy policy.

Cookies this site uses

  • Local preferences: the site stores your language choice in local storage and your Partners/Startups choice in session storage. These are browser-storage records, not advertising cookies.
  • Calendly: if the host activates the embedded meeting calendar, Calendly may use cookies or similar technologies when the booking widget loads. Calendly's own privacy and cookie terms apply inside that service.
  • No analytics currently installed: this version of the site does not load Google Analytics or another analytics tracker.

Managing cookies

You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. If you do not accept cookies, some parts of the site may not function fully.
