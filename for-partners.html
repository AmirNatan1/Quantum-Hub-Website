what you get

Scouting that starts from your needs

Need-first scouting

We do not start with a catalogue of technology. We map operational needs with partner teams, then scout globally for startups with the right stage, readiness, and fit.

POCs executed in-house

Quantum's team manages POC design, planning, and execution at the playground or an agreed partner site, with success criteria set in advance.

De-risked adoption

Success criteria are defined with the partner before testing begins. A successful POC creates evidence for adoption; an unsuccessful one creates a clear, contained learning.

clear ownership

A strong pilot begins with clear responsibility. Before testing starts, every side knows what it owns and which decision the evidence must unlock.

Partner champion

Own the operational need

  • Define the business problem and site constraints.
  • Open access to the people, data, and environment required.
  • Own the decision that follows the POC.
Quantum-hub

Own the path to evidence

  • Scout and assess the strongest-fit technologies.
  • Translate the need into scope and success criteria.
  • Coordinate execution and turn results into a decision brief.
Startup team

Own product readiness

  • Bring working technology and the technical team behind it.
  • Support integration, testing, and rapid iteration.
  • Prepare a practical route from POC to deployment.
