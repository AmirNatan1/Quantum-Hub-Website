Collecting and using your personal data

Personal data

While using Our Service, We may ask You to provide certain personally identifiable information that can be used to contact or identify You, including but not limited to: email address, first name and last name, and Usage Data.

Usage Data is collected automatically and may include Your device's IP address, browser type and version, the pages of our Service You visit, the time and date of Your visit, the time spent on those pages, unique device identifiers and other diagnostic data — including equivalent data for mobile devices.
